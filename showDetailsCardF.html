The beginning of the digital age

Companies and organizations began to realize the importance of an online presence, and thousands of websites emerged offering a variety of services, from news and e-commerce to entertainment and education. Giant companies such as Amazon (1997) and Google (1998) were founded, contributing to shaping the structure of the internet as we know it today.As the amount of information increased, the need to organize it emerged. Search engines such as Yahoo and Google emerged, becoming the gateway to the world of the Internet and facilitating access to the desired content within seconds.
